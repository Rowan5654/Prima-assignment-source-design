How to install:

Extract the folder to wherever you like.
Inside the folder title "Dreamers" there is an "index.html" file
Double click this to start the program.

The instructions are shown during the game but here they are again:

Instructions:
Move with 'w', 'a', 's', 'd'.
Shoot with 'left mouse'.
Avoid enemies or else you lose health.
5 Health is rewarded at the end of every wave.
Survive 5 waves of enemies to win!
Note: Health will not be given for skipping waves
